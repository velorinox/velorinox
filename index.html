Why is Meghalaya the Wettest?

The Bay of Bengal SW monsoon funnels into the Khasi Hills. The funnel-shaped terrain forces air upward — it cools, condenses, and dumps rain (orographic effect). Mawsynram sits right at the funnel's narrowest point.
